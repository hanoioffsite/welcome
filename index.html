Survival Kit

Before You Land

Six things to know before you touch down in Hanoi.

01 · Office

The Grand Terra, 36 Cat Linh — 7th floor. QR at elevator. Map ↗

02 · Traffic

Cross slowly — never stop halfway. Use Grab / Be / XanhSM. Avoid 7–9 AM & 5–7 PM. Old Quarter best on foot.

03 · Belongings

Front pockets in Old Quarter & Ta Hien. No valuables on cyclos. Hotel safe for passports; carry a photocopy.

04 · Cash

QR pay at cafes & malls. Apple Pay at chains. Street vendors prefer cash — carry 10K–100K VND notes.

05 · Weather

30–37°C, humidity 80–90%. Afternoon storms — always carry an umbrella. SPF 50+, cotton/linen.

06 · Phrases

Xin chào — Hello · Cảm ơn — Thank you · Bao nhiêu? — How much? · Ngon quá! — Delicious!

Experiences

Sightseeing

Rise before the sun. Walk slowly around Hoan Kiem Lake — locally called Guom Lake, the Lake of the Returned Sword — as mist lifts and willows whisper. Then a hidden café, a cup of cà phê trứng, and a steaming bowl of phở. That's how Hanoi wakes.

If the weekend finds you still here, wander through a thousand years of Hanoi — old temples, imperial gates, hidden pagodas — then lose yourself in the Old Quarter's 36 Streets.

Or trade the city for quiet: escape to Tam Coc, Ninh Binh — the river valley from Kong: Skull Island.
